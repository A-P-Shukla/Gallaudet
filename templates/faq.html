About the Technology

What is the core technology behind Gallaudet?

Gallaudet is powered by a proprietary machine learning model based on advanced computer vision algorithms. When you activate the service, your browser's WebRTC API securely accesses your camera feed. This video stream is processed ephemerally, frame by frame, using our deep learning model to analyze and identify ASL handshapes. The entire process is optimized for low-latency, real-time performance.

Which sign languages and vocabulary are supported?

Our initial model is trained and optimized for the 26-letter American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet for the purpose of finger-spelling. This is the foundational first step in our development roadmap. We are actively collecting data and developing models to support a wider vocabulary of common ASL signs and, eventually, other regional sign languages like Indian Sign Language (ISL).

Data Privacy & Compliance

How does Gallaudet handle my data and video stream?

Your privacy is architected into our system. We do not record, store, or transmit your video stream. The translation process is ephemeral, meaning the video frames are analyzed on-the-fly and immediately discarded. The content of your translated conversations is never written to a disk or a database. We operate on a principle of data minimization, collecting only what is essential to provide the service.

How do you comply with data protection laws?

As a company based in India, we are designed to comply with the principles of India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. We also adhere to the core tenets of international standards like the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for our global users. This includes:

  • Consent: We require your explicit consent to our Terms and Privacy Policy before account creation.
  • Purpose Limitation: We only use your data to provide and improve the Gallaudet service as described.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect essential account and metadata, never the content of your translations.
  • User Rights: You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data, which you can manage from your Account Settings page.

For a complete legal overview, please review our official Privacy Policy.

Account & Usage

How can I get the best translation results?

Model performance is directly influenced by the quality of the video input. For optimal results:

  1. Lighting: Use diffuse, front-facing light. Avoid strong backlighting (like sitting in front of a window) or harsh shadows.
  2. Background: A plain, non-moving background provides the best contrast for your hands.
  3. Framing: Ensure your hands and face are fully visible and centered in the frame.
  4. Clarity: Sign at a deliberate, natural pace. Unclear handshapes or very rapid transitions can impact accuracy.
How do I delete my account and data?

You have full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated personal information from your Account Settings page. Please be aware that this action is irreversible and will remove all your usage history and account details from our systems.
