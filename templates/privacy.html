Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 31, 2025

At Gallaudet ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is the cornerstone of our service. This Privacy Policy details how we handle your information when you use our website and services (collectively, the "Service"). It is designed to be transparent, technically accurate, and respectful of your data protection rights under global standards, including the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA).

Our Core Privacy Promise: Ephemeral Processing

The most sensitive data we interact with the video feed from your camera is processed ephemerally. This means the data is processed in real-time for immediate translation and is never stored, saved, or logged on our servers or your device. The content of your signed conversations is yours and yours alone; we do not want it, and we have built our systems specifically not to retain it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the minimum necessary information, which we categorize as follows:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and chosen username. We also store a cryptographically salted and hashed version of your password. We never store your password in a readable format.
  • Google Authentication: If you sign up using Google, we receive your email address from Google to create and authenticate your account. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Communications: When you contact our support team, we collect the content of those communications to assist you.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Session Metadata: We automatically log non-personal, technical information about your translation sessions. This includes the start time, end time, and calculated duration (in seconds) of each session. This metadata is crucial for providing your session log on your dashboard and for us to analyze service usage, identify performance issues, and make improvements.
  • Usage Data: We may collect standard technical information such as your device's IP address, browser type, and operating system for security purposes and to ensure compatibility.

2. Legal Basis and Purpose for Processing Data

We process your data based on the following legal grounds, in compliance with GDPR and DPDPA principles:

  • Performance of a Contract: We process your Account Data and Session Metadata to provide you with the core functionalities of the Service you signed up for, such as authenticating you, displaying your session log, and maintaining your account.
  • Legitimate Interest: We process Usage Data to maintain the security, stability, and performance of our infrastructure, which is a legitimate interest for both us and our users.
  • Consent: When you register, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

3. How We Use and Share Information

Your trust is paramount. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

  • To Operate the Service: Your data is used strictly to provide, maintain, and secure the Service.
  • With Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party services for essential operations like cloud hosting and email delivery. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements and are only permitted to use the information to perform services on our behalf.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes, such as a court order from a competent authority within the jurisdiction of India.

4. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. This includes:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • Password Hashing: We use the robust bcrypt algorithm for password hashing.
  • Data Minimization: We are committed to collecting only the data that is essential to operate the Service.

We retain your Account Data as long as your account is active. Session Metadata may be retained for analytical purposes. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your associated personal data from our primary systems.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have control over your personal data. We provide the tools to exercise your rights:

  • Right to Access & Rectify: You can access and update your account information (like your username) directly on your Account Settings page.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can permanently delete your account and all associated personal data from the Account Settings page.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.

6. International Data Transfers

As Gallaudet is based in India, your information will be processed and stored in compliance with Indian law, including the DPDPA. If you are accessing our Service from other regions (like the European Union), please be aware that your data may be transferred to, and stored in, India.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

8. Policy Updates and Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our Service. Your continued use of the Service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your rights, please do not hesitate to contact our Data Grievance Officer:

  • Email: privacy@gallaudetapp.com (Example Email)
  • Via our website: Contact Page
