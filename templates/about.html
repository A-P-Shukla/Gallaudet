Our Mission

To empower everyone to be heard.

We are driven by a simple yet powerful belief: that communication is a fundamental human right. Our mission is to bridge communication gaps by creating technology that is accessible, intuitive, and fosters understanding.

Our Story

“Gallaudet began not in a boardroom, but from a simple observation: a conversation between a hearing and a non-hearing individual that relied entirely on a notepad. We saw the patience, but also the delay. We saw the desire to connect, but also the technological gap. In that moment, we knew we could do more. We asked ourselves: what if we could build a bridge made of code, one that could make conversations flow as naturally on a screen as they do in person? That question is what drives us every single day.”

The Founders of Gallaudet

The Technology Behind the Translation

We use a combination of cutting-edge technologies to deliver a seamless experience.

Real-Time Computer Vision

Our application processes video directly in your browser, analyzing hand movements frame-by-frame without ever storing the footage.

AI & Deep Learning

Our models are trained on extensive datasets to recognize the nuances of ASL, ensuring high accuracy and performance.

Privacy by Design

We believe security is paramount. We built our entire system on the principle of never storing sensitive conversation data.
